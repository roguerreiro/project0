But... who is Taylor Swift?

Taylor Swift is a renowned American singer-songwriter who grew up in the Tennessean countryside.

She started as a country singer and moved on to become one of the biggest pop stars in recent years.

Curiosities!

  • She wrote her first song, titled "Lucky You", at age 12.
  • Her debut album was released when she was 16 years old.
  • She owns the most awarded country (Fearless) and pop (1989) albums in history.
  • Two of her albums (Speak Now and Lover) had all of their songs on the Billboard Hot 100 chart on the week of their release.
  • In 2014, she became the first female artist to replace her own song as first place on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, with "Shake It Off" and "Blank Space".
  • She has the most top 10 debuts in the Billboard Hot 100 chart, with 12 debuts.
  • Her music video for "Blank Space" became the first video by a solo female artist to reach 1 billion views on YouTube.
